IR8


IR8 rice, developed in the 1960s, is known as "miracle rice" because it grows fast and produces a high yield. It played a big role in fighting food shortages and improving rice farming worldwide.

1001


1001 rice is a high-quality, fragrant rice variety known for its soft texture and delicious taste. It's commonly used in biryanis and other flavorful dishes due to its long grains and pleasant aroma. This rice cooks well, staying fluffy and non-sticky, making it a favorite choice for everyday meals.

IR64


IR64 rice is a widely used long-grain variety known for its soft texture and mild aroma. It cooks fluffy and is commonly used for daily meals, including steamed rice, curries, and fried rice.

Sona Masoori


Sona Masoori rice is a popular medium-grain variety known for its lightweight, soft texture, and mild aroma. It is commonly used for everyday meals like steamed rice, idli, dosa, and pulao.

RNR RAW RICE


RNR Raw Rice is a premium variety known for its long grains, soft texture, and high nutritional value. It is widely used for everyday cooking, including biryani, steamed rice, and other traditional dishes.

BOILED RICE


Boiled rice, also known as parboiled rice, is partially boiled in its husk before milling. This process helps retain nutrients, making it more nutritious than regular white rice. It has a firmer texture and is less sticky, making it ideal for dishes like idli, dosa, and biryani.
